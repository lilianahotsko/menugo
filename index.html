The problem

Dining out is full of invisible friction.

Every visit hides four pain points that frustrate diners and cost restaurants revenue.

📢
Noisy & Busy Places
Shouting your order over the noise is stressful for diners and error-prone for staff — especially in packed venues.
🌐
Language Barriers
Tourists and non-native speakers struggle to understand menus, leading to wrong orders, frustration, and lost revenue.
⏳
The Waiting Game
Waiting to be seated, waiting for a server, waiting to order — it adds up. Dead time that erodes the whole experience.
⚠️
Hidden Allergens
200K+ emergency room visits per year in North America alone are caused by unlabeled or misunderstood allergens in restaurant food.
